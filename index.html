Salvador Soto
I’m a Product Designer focused on Rapid Prototyping and Motion Design, I like designing, coding, and tinkering. More about me.
Formed as an Industrial Designer, I have served as a UX Designer, Rapid Prototyper, and Design Technologist within Innovation and Research & Development at Flextronics, Hisense, and Katerra.

My past experiences involve; The creation of a hybrid Dual-Screen Smartphone at Flextronics. Conceptualizing and prototyping multiple Touch, D-Pad, and Voice User Interfaces, developed into full 10-foot User Interfaces, Smart displays, and TV products for Hisense, Toshiba, and Sharp. The creation of UI Design Systems and enterprise applications for Katerra's Advanced Buildings and IoT products ecosystem.

Over my 10 past years of experience I've gained insightful experience by working together with multiple teams across different time-zones, languages, and regions, such as Mexico, US, Canada, India, and China.

My contributions have listed me as a registered inventor in multiple technology & innovation patents, and rewarded me with final positions in international design contests such the Index Project Award.

I'm very passionate about designing and prototyping new concepts, innovative experiences, and meaningful solutions, driven by my immense curiosity to understand and explore multiple disciplines outside my work.

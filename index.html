Salvador Soto
Designer
Formed as an Industrial Designer, I have served as a Product Designer, Rapid Prototyper, and Creative Technologist within Innovation and Research & Development areas while working at Flextronics, Hisense, and Katerra.

My past experiences involve: The creation of a hybrid dual-screen smartphone at Flextronics. Designing and prototyping touch, d-pad, and voice driven interfaces grown into the development of multiple 10-foot display UI's for Hisense, Toshiba, and Sharp products. Multiple in-house tools, components, and applications for Katerra building management and internal IoT ecosystem.

During this time, I've gained insightful experience by working together with multiple teams across different time-zones, languages, and regions, such as Mexico, US, Canada, India, and China.

My contributions have listed me as a registered inventor in multiple technology & innovation patents, and rewarded me with final positions in design contests such as Michelin and Index Project Award.

I'm very passionate about designing and prototyping new concepts, innovative experiences, and meaningful solutions driven by my immense curiosity to understand and explore many disciplines outside my work.

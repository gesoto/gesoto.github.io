Within the Hisense Innovation group, my work compromised multiple conceptual proposals involving a wide range of skills, from Visual, Motion, and Animation Design, to High fidelity prototyping, Front-end Development, and even Hardware prototyping.

While in charge of Rapid Prototyping I used as many platforms and tools as possible in order to achieve a final product like experience during the very early stages of the project. This allowed for essential user testing, pre-development design changes, and as a unified vision to the entire group and stakeholders.

Many products ended up being successfully released into the international market under the Sharp & Hisense umbrella.

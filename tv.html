Within the Hisense Innovation group, my work compromised multiple conceptual proposals involving a wide range of design + development skills.

While in charge of rapid prototyping I used as many platforms and tools as possible in order to achieve end-product like experiences during the early stages of the project development. This allowed for essential user testing, pre-development design changes, and a unified vision to multiple stakeholders.
